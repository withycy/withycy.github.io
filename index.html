Hello, I'm Chenchang Yi

I am a travel photographer living in San Francisco Bay Area. I have a profound passion for capturing the essence of the world through the lens of my camera.

Galley Awards

Landscapes are my sweet spot, but I also love diving into cityscapes, the serenity of nature, and even the cosmos through astrophotography. I'm also exploring taking videos with my camera and drone.

I've been horoned to win some international photography awards along the way. Whether it's a sweeping vista, a bustling city, or the quietude of nature, I'm here to share the magic of the world through my lens.
